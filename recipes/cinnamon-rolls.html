Cinnamon Rolls

Description

This easy cinnamon roll recipe is one of the most popular recipes on my website, for good reason. These are classic, homestyle cinnamon rolls. It took me lots of recipe testing trial-and-error to develop quick cinnamon rolls that maintain all the flavor and texture of traditional overnight cinnamon rolls. But THIS. IS. IT.And I promise, making these easy cinnamon rolls is 100x more satisfying than that feeling you get from popping open a store-bought can of ready-to-bake rolls.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Whole milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
